About Us

Serving Humanity with
Compassion & Purpose

Hayat Aid Organization is a nonprofit charity based in Iganga, Uganda, dedicated to transforming lives through impactful Islamic humanitarian services.

We support orphans, build mosques and schools, distribute Qurans, provide clean water, and deliver food and mobility support to those most in need.

Learn more

Our Core Projects

Orphan Feeding

We provide nutritious meals to orphans, ensuring their health and happiness.

Water Well Construction

We dig boreholes and water wells to provide clean, safe drinking water to entire villages.

Food Pack Distribution

We distribute food packages filled with daily essentials to struggling families.

Quran Distribution

We supply Qurans to madrasas and new Muslims to support spiritual learning.

About Us

Serving Humanity with Compassion, One Project at a Time

Hayat Aid Organization is a non-profit charity based in Iganga, Uganda, dedicated to uplifting the lives of the poor, orphans, disabled, and underserved communities through impactful, faith-rooted humanitarian projects.

Since our founding, we've worked tirelessly to ensure that every meal shared, Quran distributed, well constructed, or mosque built not only meets a need — but restores dignity and hope.

Our Mission

To serve humanity through compassionate, sustainable, and faith-aligned charity programs that improve lives and empower communities.

Our Vision

A world where every person—regardless of background or location—has access to food, education, water, spiritual growth, and opportunity.

CORE VALUES

1.

Compassion

We prioritize empathy and kindness, understanding the struggles of others. Our actions are guided by a deep commitment to serving humanity, fostering a culture of care, and providing support to those in need with dignity and respect.

2.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability, ensuring our words and actions align. Our financial practices, decision-making processes, and relationships are built on trust, ethics, and a strong moral compass, reflecting our commitment to integrity.

3.

Inclusivity

We believe in the inherent worth of every individual, serving diverse communities without bias. Our initiatives promote equality, respect, and opportunities for all, embracing the richness of different backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives to create a more just and compassionate world.

4.

Excellence

We strive for quality, efficiency, and effectiveness in all we do, setting high standards and continuously improving. Our commitment to excellence ensures our programs and services make a meaningful impact, empowering individuals and communities to thrive.

5.

Sustainability

We prioritize long-term impact and responsible stewardship, ensuring our initiatives are environmentally conscious and financially viable. By building lasting relationships and sustainable solutions, we create a brighter future for generations to come.

What We Do

1. Orphan Feeding

Providing nutritious meals to orphans and vulnerable children.

2. Qurban Sacrifice

Distributing meat during Eid to families in need.

3. Quran Distribution

Supplying Qurans to schools and new Muslims.

4. Clean Water Projects

Building wells and water systems for communities in need.

5. Educational Support

Providing scholarships and school supplies to students.

6. Medical Aid

Offering healthcare services and medical equipment to those in need.

Why We Exist

In many parts of Eastern Uganda, communities suffer silently — without clean water, education, or a place to pray.

We exist to change that reality through Islamic charity and community-driven solutions.

Who We Serve

  • Orphans and widows
  • Rural and low-income communities
  • People with disabilities
  • Quran schools and madrasa students
  • Villages without access to clean water or prayer spaces

Our Projects

Changing Lives Through Compassion and Action

At HAYAT AID ORGANIZATION, we don't just talk about making a difference — we do it.

Every project we run is a direct response to the needs of the communities we serve in Iganga and surrounding areas.

01.

Quran Distribution

Overview

The Quran is more than just a book—it's guidance, mercy, and a source of light in our darkest moments. In Ramadan, the month of the Quran, every letter recited brings multiplied blessings.
But for many, this priceless gift is out of reach. In many communities, children and adults share a single, worn-out Quran—waiting for their turn to hold the words of Allah (SWT) in their hands. Your gift can change that.
We distribute printed Qurans to mosques, madrasas and homes so people can have the words of Allah (SWT) at their fingertips and in their hearts.

02.

Qurban Distribution

Overview

We extend our gratitude to all who contributed to our Qurban project, providing essential support to communities in need. This initiative embodies the spirit of sacrifice and compassion, ensuring that those less fortunate receive nourishment and relief during Eid al-Adhuha.
Qurban plays a crucial role in spreading joy and solidarity within our communities. Your generous donations have enabled us to distribute meat to families facing hardship, allowing them to partake in the festivities and experience the blessings of Eid. Despite the success of this endeavour, ongoing support is essential to reach more beneficiaries and make a lasting impact on their lives.
As we reflect on the significance of Qurban, we are reminded of the importance of generosity and kindness towards others. Your continued contributions enable us to uphold these values and extend a helping hand to those in need, fostering unity and compassion within our society.

03.

Quran Distribution Initiative

Overview

Embracing the spiritual heart of our community, we're actively involved in building, revitalizing, and upholding mosques. These centers of devotion serve as vital hubs for learning, prayer, and unity.
From erecting new mosques to preserving historical ones and providing ongoing care, we're dedicated to ensuring these spaces inspire peace and connection. Your support empowers us to nurture sacred environments that foster faith and togetherness.
By joining us, you contribute to the lasting impact of these spiritual landmarks, touching lives and fostering a sense of belonging. Let's unite to create spaces where faith thrives and communities flourish.
